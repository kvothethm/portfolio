About Me

My name is Jeff, and I have recently earned a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from The Evergreen State College. I am transitioning to tech after a 20-year career in the U.S. Army as a Human Intelligence Collector. Since graduating, I have been working as a Web Developer Intern at Evergreen. I work on the EDURange project writing React and Flask code to create forms, databases, and the logic that moves data between them. I have been working with industry standard technologies such as Git, PostgreSQL, Node.js, Nginx, and, of course, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
